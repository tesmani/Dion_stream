What is the difference between a device connection and a stream on Dion_stream?
A device connection is how many devices you set up with your Dion_stream account. A stream is how many devices can watch at the same time. If you configure 3 devices but have 1 stream, only one device can play live TV or VOD at once. To watch on multiple screens simultaneously, choose a plan with more devices/streams.
Why does the Dion_stream order pop-up ask for my name and email before payment?

What exactly is IPTV?
IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television. Instead of satellite or classic cable, channels are delivered through the internet. Dion_stream uses IPTV technology so you can watch on many devices using your connection.
